<h2 class="heading-title">Support to Pay Energy Bills</h2>

<p class="heading-description">
  Organizations and resources that can help if you're struggling with energy costs.
</p>

<h3>Household Support Fund</h3>
<p>
  Government fund administered by Bristol City Council. You need to be referred by a practitioner (e.g. a social worker) or by an advice service such as Citizens Advice.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="https://www.bristol.gov.uk/residents/benefits/help-with-one-off-payments-and-grants" target="_blank">Bristol City Council Support</a><br>
  <strong>Phone:</strong> 0117 922 2000
</p>

<h3>Citizens Advice Bristol</h3>
<p>
  Contact via website for advice on energy bills, benefits, and debt management.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="https://www.citizensadvice.org.uk/local/bristol" target="_blank">www.citizensadvice.org.uk/local/bristol</a><br>
  <strong>Phone:</strong> 0808 278 7900
</p>

<h3>Trussell Trust 'Help through Hardship' Helpline</h3>
<p>
  Information on claiming additional benefits, grants and support, and emergency financial help.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.trussell.org.uk/help-and-advice/help-through-hardship-helpline" target="_blank">www.trussell.org.uk/help-and-advice/help-through-hardship-helpline</a><br>
  <strong>Phone:</strong> 0808 208 2138
</p>

<h3>British Gas Energy Trust</h3>
<p>
  Assists with energy debt. You do not have to be a British Gas customer to apply. Need first to seek help from a debt advice service such as National Debtline or Citizens Advice, or use a self-help Benefit Calculator tool.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>National Debtline:</strong> 0808 808 4000<br>
  <strong>Citizens Advice:</strong> 0808 278 7900 (and see above)<br>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://britishgasenergytrust.org.uk/who-can-apply" target="_blank">britishgasenergytrust.org.uk/who-can-apply</a>
</p>

<h3>Turn2Us</h3>
<p>
  Online resource covering financial support through welfare benefits and grants.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.turn2us.org.uk" target="_blank">www.turn2us.org.uk</a><br>
  <strong>Energy bills info:</strong> <a href="http://www.turn2us.org.uk/get-support/information-for-your-situation/cost-of-living/struggling-with-energy-and-water-bills" target="_blank">Energy and water bills support</a><br>
  <strong>Grant search:</strong> <a href="http://grants-search.turn2us.org.uk" target="_blank">grants-search.turn2us.org.uk</a>
</p>

<h3>Age UK Advice Line</h3>
<p>
  Advice about benefits and how to claim them - e.g. can ensure you're getting Winter Fuel Payments, Warm Home Discount, Cold Weather Payments etc if you are pension age.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.ageuk.org.uk/services/age-uk-advice-line" target="_blank">www.ageuk.org.uk/services/age-uk-advice-line</a><br>
  <strong>Phone:</strong> 0800 678 1602
</p>

<h3>Disability Energy Support</h3>
<p>
  Administered by Scope. Apply by email or phone. Open to any disabled person, or households where one or more disabled people live. Can assist with managing energy debt, energy efficiency, accessing benefits and grants, etc.
</p>
<p>
  <strong>Website:</strong> <a href="http://www.scope.org.uk/disability-energy-support" target="_blank">www.scope.org.uk/disability-energy-support</a>
</p>
